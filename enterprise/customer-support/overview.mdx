<Info>
**Enterprise Only** — Support portal access is available exclusively to Portkey Enterprise customers. Can't log in? Contact [support@portkey.ai](mailto:support@portkey.ai) to request access.
</Info>

## Quick Reference

### Support Tiers

| Feature | Standard (Included) | Premium (+$2k/mo) |
|---------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Coverage | 09:00-17:00 ET, Mon-Fri | 24x7x365 |
| Channels | Portal, Email | Portal, Email, Slack, Phone |
| Named Contacts | 2 | 5 |
| Panic Button | No | Yes (SEV 1 emergencies) |

### Response Times

| Severity | Standard | Premium |
|----------|----------|---------|
| SEV 1 (Production down) | 4 business hours | 30 minutes |
| SEV 2 (Major impact) | 4 business hours | 2 hours |
| SEV 3 (Minor issue) | 1 business day | 4 hours |
| SEV 4 (General question) | 2 business days | 1 business day |
